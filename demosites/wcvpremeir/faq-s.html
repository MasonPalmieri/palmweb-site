FAQs

How fast can you make an offer?

Often within 24–48 hours after reviewing the basic property details. Timelines vary based on complexity.

Do I have to make repairs?

No. Many “as-is” options are available depending on the situation. We focus on clarity and realistic next steps.

Do you work with real estate agents?

Yes — this demo assumes collaboration is welcome. Adjust the wording based on the client’s real process.

What fees do I pay?

This depends on the deal structure. A redesigned site should clearly explain any fees and who covers closing costs.

Can you help with inherited or tenant-occupied properties?

Many buyers can — highlight this clearly if it’s part of their real offering. The key is making it easy to understand.
